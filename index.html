Sound

Crafted to bring any vision to life, with rich, upscale timbres and balanced clarity. Specializing in lo-fi and jazzy tracks that melt into the background or set the perfect boutique atmosphere. Ideal for media, branding, or immersive experiences.

Picture

Capturing the essence of the creative process, from intimate studio moments to polished product and interior photography. Combining documentary storytelling with an artistic eye to produce images that feel authentic, atmospheric, and visually striking.

Space

Bespoke solutions for functional and inspiring environments—pedalboards from reclaimed wood, custom acoustic panels, interior pillars, or complete studio builds. High-end materials like sheepswool ensure superior quality and acoustics, creating spaces that feel intentional, comfortable, and refined.

About

Tim van Veen is a sonic and visual storyteller based in Amsterdam, blending music, photography, and spatial design to craft cinematic atmospheres for boutique brands and bespoke projects. With a refined sense of aesthetics and a deep awareness of the audience’s experience, he translates a brand’s essence into every detail. Beyond concept and direction, Tim’s hands-on approach shapes the space in between—bringing ideas to life with precision, character, and atmosphere.
